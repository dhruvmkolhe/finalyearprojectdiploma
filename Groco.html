Gov Schemes

BETI BACHAO BETI PADHAO YOJANA

The key elements of the scheme include Enforcement of PC & PNDT Act, nation-wide awareness and advocacy campaign and multi-sectoral action in select 100 districts (low on CSR) in the first phase.
Apply Now

CBSE Udaan Scheme

The Central Board of Secondary Education, in collaboration with the Ministry of Human Resource Development of the Government of India, manages and administers the CBSE Udaan scheme for girls. This program aims to increase the number of girls enrolled in prestigious engineering and technical colleges across India.
Apply Now

Balika Samridhhi Yojana

The Balika Samriddhi Yojana is a scholarship initiative that provides financial support to young girls and their families who live in poverty. The scheme's key aim is to raise girls' social status, increase their marriageable age, and increase enrolment as well as girls' enrolment for school studies.
Apply Now

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Dowry and other marriage costs are two of the most common causes of sexism against girls. Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (Women Child Prosperity Account) is a government-sponsored savings program for parents of girl children.
Apply Now

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

The objective of the scheme is to augment the income of families of all Farmers who own cultivable landholding, subject to certain exclusions. The scheme was launched on 24th February, 2019 with an aim to supplement the financial needs of the Small and marginal Farmers (SMFs) .
Apply Now

National Scheme of Welfare of Fishermen.

Under this component assistance is provided @ Rs. 4500/- per fishers which includes Rs. 3000/- per fishers to be provided by the Governemntal and Rs. 1500/- to be contributed by the beneficiary for three months consisting of fishing ban/lean periods and (ii) Insurance to Fishers.
Apply Now

PM Kisan Tractor Scheme

According to the details, there has a benefit of subsidy going to provide by the central government for the purchase of a tractor. Moreover, this scheme has on a national level and has applications in all the states. The main objective of the scheme has that farmers can able to purchase a tractor for agriculture.

Unique package for farmers.

This scheme aims to ensure the availability of urea to the farmers at a subsidized price of Rs 242 per 45 kg bag, excluding taxes and neem coating charges. The total subsidy package for this period is Rs 3,68,676.7 Crore, in addition to the Nutrient Based Subsidy of Rs 38,000 Crore for the Kharif season of 2023-24.

PM-JAY

It provides a cover of Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization across public and private empanelled hospitals in India..
Apply Now

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana

The PMJAY is a government health insurance scheme for individuals belonging to the lower socio-economic strata of society. Under this scheme, each household can get medical coverage of up to ₹5 Lakh for secondary and tertiary hospitalisation care.
Apply Now

Awaz Health Insurance Scheme

The Awaz Health Insurance Scheme was launched by the Government of Kerala in 2017 with the intention of providing health insurance to inter-state labourers spread across the state. In addition to providing them with financial assistance in the case of medical emergencies, the scheme also offers an accidental death benefit to the beneficiary’s nominees.
Apply Now

Aam Aadmi Bima Yojana

Aam Aadmi Bima Yojana was launched to support employees engaged across 48 vocations, including carpentry, weaving, fishing, and so on. To be eligible for the Aam Aadmi Bima insurance scheme, you must be the breadwinner of your family and, if so, can make a claim amounting to ₹30,000 during the policy term by paying a premium of just ₹200 per annum.
Apply Now

Pradhan Mantri Aawas Yojna

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U), a flagship Mission of Government of India being implemented by Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), was launched on 25th June 2015. The Mission addresses urban housing shortage among the EWS/LIG and MIG categories including the slum dwellers by ensuring a pucca house to all eligible urban households by the year 2022, when Nation completes 75 years of its Independence.
Apply Now

Rajiv Awas Yojna (RAY)

It aims to eradicate slums from India and encourages the states and union territories to bring any illegal construction under a legal framework formally. The centre has authorised the Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP) scheme to be a part of RAY to promote affordable housing under the plan.
Apply Now

Suraksha Housing Scheme

In urban and rural areas, those who belong to the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) can receive financial aid for self-building a home (if they own at least two cents of land).
Apply Now

NTR Urban Housing Scheme

The Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao (NTR) Housing Plan was launched to assist Andhra Pradesh's poor population in acquiring inexpensive homes. You must have a BPL or white ration card to apply for the NTR housing scheme.
Apply Now
